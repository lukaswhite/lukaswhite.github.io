Gear 4 Music

Gear 4 Music are a large online retailer of musical equipment and software, who required not only a new online presence but a fully-featured e-commerce solution handling stock management and logistics. I was part of a team of developers engineering a new bespoke system to do just that, built atop of the Zend Framework and utilising ExtJS for the management screens. Areas I covered personally included stock allocation, intra-warehouse stock transfers and integration with Parcel Force's delivery and consignment systems.
